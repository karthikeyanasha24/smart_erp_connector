Privacy Policy — SmarterP ERP Connector

Last updated: 1 June 2026 · Meenabazar · Developer: Asha Karthikeyan (independent freelancer)

This Privacy Policy describes how SmarterP ERP Connector (“the Add-on”), offered for Meenabazar, collects, uses, stores, and shares information when you use the Google Sheets add-on with your organization’s SmarterP backend.

1. Who we are

Meenabazar is the organization that uses and offers this integration for its ERP analytics workflows. The Add-on is developed and maintained by Asha Karthikeyan, an independent freelancer, on behalf of Meenabazar.

Privacy & support contact: ashakarthikeyan24@gmail.com

2. What the Add-on does

The Add-on connects Google Sheets to a SmarterP ERP API server that you configure (HTTPS URL). You can pull dashboard KPIs, branches, transactions, and other reports into sheets, and send business questions to an AI feature. You enter your backend URL and login in SmarterP → Settings / Connect.

3. Information the Add-on accesses

  • Google user identity: Used by Google to run the Add-on in your account. We do not receive your Google password.
  • Current spreadsheet: The Add-on reads and writes only the spreadsheet you have open (new tabs such as Dashboard KPIs, Transactions, or AI result sheets). It does not browse or modify other Drive files.
  • Connection settings you provide: Backend URL, email, and password are saved in Google Apps Script Script Properties (Google-hosted storage tied to the script). They are used only to sign in to your SmarterP API and obtain a session token.
  • ERP / business data: Sales, branch, product, transaction, and similar data returned by your SmarterP server is shown in Sheets or the sidebar. This data comes from your ERP database via your backend, not from Google.
  • AI questions: Text you type in the AI tab is sent to your backend endpoint /ai/query for processing on your server.

4. How we use information

We use the information above only to:

  • Authenticate to your configured SmarterP backend
  • Fetch and display ERP reports you request
  • Write results into your spreadsheet when you use Pull or “Add to Sheet”
  • Operate and improve the Add-on and related Meenabazar systems

We do not sell, rent, or use Google user data or sheet contents for advertising. We do not use Google user data for purposes unrelated to providing this Add-on.

5. Google API Services — Limited Use

SmarterP ERP Connector’s use of information received from Google APIs complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements:

  • We use Google data only to provide the Add-on features you request.
  • We do not transfer Google user data to third parties except as needed to operate the Add-on (e.g. your own SmarterP server), comply with law, or with your consent.
  • We do not use Google user data for ads.
  • Human access to Google user data is limited to security, support, and legal needs, or with your agreement.

OAuth scopes:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly — read/write the active spreadsheet
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.container.ui — show the sidebar and settings dialog
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.external_request — HTTPS requests to your SmarterP backend only

6. Where data is stored

  • Google: Spreadsheet content and Script Properties are stored by Google under your Google Workspace / Google account terms.
  • Your SmarterP backend: ERP data and API logs are processed on the server URL you configure (for example, a cloud host such as Render). Meenabazar controls that deployment.

7. Sharing with others

We may share information only in these cases:

  • Google — to run Sheets and Apps Script
  • Your SmarterP API — to fulfill your requests (login, reports, AI)
  • Infrastructure providers — hosting for the backend (under confidentiality obligations)
  • Legal — if required by applicable law

We do not share Google user data with data brokers or for unrelated marketing.

8. Security

Communication with your backend uses HTTPS. Use strong passwords, limit backend accounts to authorized staff, and clear Add-on settings when you stop using the integration. Revoke access at Google Account permissions if needed.

9. Retention

Connection settings remain in Script Properties until you delete them in Settings or remove the Add-on. Sheet data remains in your spreadsheet until you delete it. Backend retention follows Meenabazar’s internal policies.

10. Your rights and choices

  • Stop using the Add-on and revoke Google access at any time
  • Clear stored URL and credentials in SmarterP → Settings
  • Email ashakarthikeyan24@gmail.com for privacy questions or requests about data we control on the backend

11. Children

The Add-on is for business users. It is not directed at children under 16.

12. Changes

We may update this policy. The “Last updated” date will change. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.
